Events and Markets

Weekly Markets

Held every Wednesday and Saturday in the Market House, offering a variety of local products. Arrive early for the best selection of fresh produce and handmade crafts.

Tetbury Music Festival

Held annually in the autumn, featuring classical music performances by renowned artists. Enjoy concerts in the beautiful setting of St Mary’s Church and other local venues.

Christmas in Tetbury

Experience the festive atmosphere with Christmas markets, lights, and events throughout December. The annual Christmas lights switch-on event is a local favorite.

Woolsack Races

Held every May, the Woolsack Races are a traditional event dating back hundreds of years. Participants race up and down Gumstool Hill carrying a heavy sack of wool. The event also includes live music, food stalls, and a lively atmosphere.

Tetbury Food and Drink Festival

Taking place in September, this festival celebrates local food and drink producers. The event includes cooking demonstrations, tastings, and a variety of food stalls showcasing the best of Tetbury’s culinary offerings.

Tetbury Classic Car Show

Held in late summer, the Tetbury Classic Car Show features a stunning collection of vintage and classic cars. Car enthusiasts from across the region gather to showcase their prized vehicles, making it a must-visit for auto lovers.

Tetbury Summer Show

Occurring in August, the Tetbury Summer Show is a traditional country show with a variety of competitions, including best flowers, vegetables, and crafts. The event also features family-friendly activities, entertainment, and food stalls.
