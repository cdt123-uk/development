Dining

Cafes and Tearooms

Lyndseys and The Blue Zucchini Brasserie offer delightful options for breakfast and lunch.

Pubs and Restaurants

The Snooty Fox and The Ormond at Tetbury provide hearty meals and local ales in a warm ambiance.

Sweet Treats

The Sweet Pea Cafe & More is known for its delicious cakes, and The Priory Inn has an excellent dessert menu.
